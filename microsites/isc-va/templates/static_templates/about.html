About UTAustinX Professional Education

UTAustinX Professional Education is offering high-quality learning experiences for institutions and organizations seeking rigorous and certified training for their employees and for individuals seeking to advance their own careers.

UTAustinX Professional Education facilitates Continuing Education Units (CEUs) through our courses offered. UT Austin faculty members create and teach every course and add their own brand of excellence to every offering. Students may choose one of the three CEU granting agencies listed below:

  • The Center for Lifelong Engineering Education (CLEE) in the Cockrell School of Engineering for engineering CEUs
  • The Texas Executive Education in the McCombs School of Business for business CEUs
  • The Petroleum Extension Service (PETEX) at the University of Texas at Austin for oil and gas industry CEUs

Please consult with your professional organization or corporate human resources department to determine which affiliation has more value for your career. Students will be prompted at registration to decide from which organization they would like to receive CEUs.

University credit is not currently offered for UTAustinX Professional Education courses. Several on campus organizations offer online and distance courses for credit. Visit www.utexas.edu for more information. The UT Austin website features information about undergraduate admissions, graduate admissions, and different on campus and distance education resources available.
