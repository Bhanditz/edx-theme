GENERAL

Who can take a Texas Gateway course?
Texas Gateway courses are open to all Texas teachers.

How much does it cost to take a course?
Texas Gateway courses are free for all Texas teachers.

How do I sign up to begin taking courses in the Texas Gateway?
To sign up, complete this registration form. Once you submit the form to create your account, you will receive an activation email. You must click on the activation link in that email to complete the process.

What browsers are supported by the Texas Gateway?
The Texas Gateway works best with current versions of Chrome, Firefox or Safari, or with Internet Explorer version 9 and above.

Can I access the Texas Gateway on my mobile device?
The Texas Gateway was designed to work with most major mobile devices, including smartphones and tablets.

What if my question isn’t answered here?
Contact us by submitting your question to our help desk.

TECHNICAL TROUBLE

What if I have technical trouble with registration, login, or course access?
Many problems with registration, login, or access to courses are caused by browser issues. Here are some things to try:

  • Switch to a current version of Chrome, Safari or Firefox.
  • Check your browser settings to ensure both JavaScript and cookies are enabled.
  • Clear your browser's cache.

What if I forget my password?
Click on the “Forgot password?” link above the password field on the login page. Enter your email address when prompted and we will send you instructions for setting a new password.

What if I did not receive an activation email or password reset email?
If you did not receive an activation email or password reset email, it may be because of the following::

  • There was a typo in your email address.
  • Your spam filter may have caught the email. Please check your spam folder.
  • You may be using an older browser. We recommend downloading the current version of Firefox or Chrome.
  • JavaScript is disabled in your browser. Please check your browser settings and confirm that JavaScript is enabled.
  • You have not successfully registered a Texas Gateway account. Please try registering again with the same email address. (If an account already exists, you'll see an error regarding a duplicate email address.)
If you continue to have problems activating your account, we recommend that you try creating a new account.

What if I have problems with the display?
The problems may be caused by your browser or settings. Try the following:

  • Switch to a current version of Chrome, Safari or Firefox.
  • Check your browser settings to ensure both JavaScript and cookies are enabled.

COURSES

When does my course start and/or finish?
You can find the start date and course duration for each course on its course description page. If you are registered for a course, you'll also see its start date listed in the course information under “Current Courses” on your dashboard.

What does it mean if the start date is “Anytime, Self-Paced”?
Many Texas Gateway courses are self-paced. Self-paced courses can be taken anytime after the course is released. You can move through the course at your own pace, completing the lessons as quickly or as slowly as you like.

How do I register for a course?
Click on the course you are interested in to view more information about the course. Under the course title, you will see a button with “Register for [Course Number]” on it. Click that button to complete the registration process.

How do I access the course I am registered for?
Log into your Texas Gateway account at http://texasgatewaypd.org/login. This will take you to your dashboard, where you will see a list of your courses. Click “View Course” next to the course you would like to access.

How much work will I have to do to pass my course?
Courses have different assignments and each will take a different amount of time to complete. CPE hours for each course are usually included on the course description page. Check the course info page and course syllabus for more details about the work required for a course.

How can I find my course discussion forum posts?
You can locate all posts that you have added and contributed to in the discussion forum of your course by entering your username in the search bar. You will then see the options to view all of your posts.

How are certificates determined?
Each student who receives a passing grade for a course will receive a certificate. The assignments and passing grade are different for each course - check the course description page or the course syllabus to learn more about how to pass a specific course.

Can I take more than one course at a time?
Yes. There is no limit to the number of courses you can take at one time.

How do I unenroll from a course?
To unenroll, find your course in your dashboard. Click the small settings gear next to the “View Course” button and select “Unenroll.” Click the “Unenroll” button in the confirmation message that appears.
