The Texas Gateway provides statewide online professional development opportunities for Texas teachers. Through the Texas Gateway, teachers have anytime, anywhere access to trainings that offer

  • extensive instruction in the TEKS, the ELPS, and the RtI model;
  • strategies on providing effective instructional support for English language learners, Gifted and Talented students, and struggling students; and
  • CPE credits and certifications.

Texas Gateway courses are all TEA-approved and available at no cost to practicing and pre-service teachers across the state. Teachers simply enroll to get instant access to courses where they can watch instructional videos, engage in interactive activities, participate in discussions, and earn CPE credits.
